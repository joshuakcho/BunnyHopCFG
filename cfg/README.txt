====== Auto Bunnyhopping with Max Velocity and Air Acceleration ======

- Changed air veloicty vector caps and acceleration for max bunny hopping speed
- Enabled auto bunnyhopping through holding space
- Incorporated editable bind to use for auto jumping
- All commands editable in script



====== To use: ======

- Put bhop.cfg in ...\Steam\steamapps\common\Counter-Strike Global Offensive\csgo\cfg or take what you want from it and add to your autoexec config!
- Enter launch options at Steam > Library > Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (right-click) > Properties > Set Launch Options...
- Add "+exec bhop.cfg" to launch options
- In console, enter "exec bhop"
